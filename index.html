About This Event

The Atlanta Summer Farmers Market is your premier destination for fresh, locally sourced produce, artisan foods, handcrafted goods, and community spirit. Held at Patterson Park in southwest Atlanta, this three-day market brings together vendors from across Georgia.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply as a vendor? ▼
Click "Apply as Vendor" on the event page and complete the form. Once accepted, you'll receive an email and have 72 hours to submit payment via Chime to $Laura-Ramirez-195.
What are the booth prices? ▼
Service/Product Vendors: $65/day (10×10), $70/day (10×20), $90/day (Food Truck). Food Vendors: $73/day (10×10), $78/day (10×20), $90/day (Food Truck). All prices are per day.
How do I pay? ▼
All payments are made through Chime only. Send payment to $Laura-Ramirez-195 and upload a screenshot of the successful transaction in the application portal.
What is the cancellation/refund policy? ▼
Vendor fees are non-refundable once payment is confirmed. If the event is canceled due to rain, a decision will be made by 7 AM and you'll be notified by text.
What time can vendors set up? ▼
Vendors have 2 hours before the event opens to set up. Tables and chairs are provided. You're responsible for your own signage and keeping your booth clean.
